---
title: "Referral Program"
description: "Share Sintra and earn free months"
---

Sintra offers a simple referral program. Click the gift icon on the left sidebar to open a modal titled "Give and get 1 month free." 

The modal displays:
- Your referral code (e.g., T1WKS)
- Instructions to copy and share it with friends
- Details about the reward (both you and your friend receive one month of Sintra X free when they sign up with your code)

Use the Copy button to copy the code to your clipboard. The program may change, so check the modal for current terms.
