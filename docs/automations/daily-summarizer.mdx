---
title: "Daily Summarizer"
description: "Get daily summaries from your email and calendar"
---


The Daily Summarizer collects information from email and calendar accounts and generates a summary at a time you choose. 

## Setup Steps

1. Select Daily Summarizer on the Automations page.
2. Choose your data sources: Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar and/or Outlook Calendar.
3. Pick a time of day when you want your summary delivered (e.g., 8 AM daily).
4. Click Save. 

Sintra will begin sending a daily digest with highlights such as meetings, important emails and tasks.

## Note

This automation may require connecting your email and calendar accounts through integrations.
